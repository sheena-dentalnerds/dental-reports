📋 Executive Summary

Cloud 9 Dental of Encino is a modern general and cosmetic dental practice led by Dr. Yehonatan "Yoni" Mizrahi, DDS — a Dean's List graduate of USC's Ostrow School of Dentistry with dual dental and hygiene degrees. The practice has a strong brand identity, a well-built WordPress website, active social media, and glowing patient testimonials. However, the practice faces significant competitive pressure in one of LA's most dentist-saturated markets (2,519+ dentists within 10 miles), limited weekday hours (closed Mondays, no weekend availability), and a critical gap in high-value service offerings like dental implants and Invisalign. Review volume is likely below the top Encino competitors, and the practice lacks the local SEO architecture needed to dominate neighborhood-level searches across the 6+ communities it serves.
