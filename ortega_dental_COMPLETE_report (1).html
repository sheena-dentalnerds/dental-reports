🔍 SEO Audit Findings
Technical SEO Issues
CRITICAL: Entire site built on Wix — known for poor crawlability, slow page load, JavaScript rendering problems, and inability to implement advanced schema markup. Platform migration is the single highest-impact action available.
CRITICAL: No dedicated high-value service landing pages — "dental implants San Juan Capistrano" gets 200+ monthly searches with zero dedicated page to capture that traffic.
WARNING: TikTok link on site directs to Wix's own TikTok account (not the practice's) — all visitors clicking social links are led away from the brand entirely.
WARNING: Zero blog or educational content — competitors can outrank on long-tail queries like "PRF bone grafting vs traditional" that Ortega Dental has earned the authority to own.
WARNING: No LocalBusiness or Dentist schema markup — competitor sites with schema get rich results (star ratings, hours, phone) displayed directly in Google search, increasing click-through rates significantly.
